Tired of the never-ending rise in heating fuel costs?

So are we. That’s why Joe the Plumber has become a leader in the installation of Solar Thermal Energy Systems. Harnessing the sun’s power for your water-heating needs not only reduces your carbon footprint, but saves you money. Studies show that households and businesses can save as much as 75% on their water-heating costs with the right solar thermal system. Some of our solar heating services include:

  • Site evaluation
  • System design and installation
  • Service, repair, and replacement of system equipment
  • Help with obtaining grants, rebates, and state and federal energy tax credits
